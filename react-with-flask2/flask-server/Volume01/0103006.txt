The R. C. Church demands of its Pope, Bishops and Priests... The R. C. Church demands of its Pope, Bishops and Priests celibacy in the extreme ; very good, much like Jesus. Now, a curious anomaly is here made apparent: a pure head—if celibacy intends purity— and a corrupt body—if marriage, and fleshly lusts are the opposite of celibacy. How can the laity hope, or much less expect to go, where their pure-minded priests are rewarded? 'I am the way.'
