Deceased J. Rufus Bryant J. Rufus Bryant , Pleasant Hill, Ky., went to rest on May 6th. He was a bright light in Zion's upbuilding. As a Trustee, he had few equals in all that pertains to consecrated temporal aid and endeavor. We give Elder Harvey Eades' announcement : ' J. B. Bryant went to Heaven on the 6th.''
