Deaths. Charlotte Priest Charlotte Priest at Harvard, Mass. May 25, 1891. Age 90 yrs. 1 mo. and 13 days. Sister Charlotte has had a long experience. The spirit of purity was a marked characteristic in all her actions. She came into the Community in early life and leaves a clean record of 'Well done, good and faithful servant, enter thou into the joy of thy Lord.' A. D. B.
