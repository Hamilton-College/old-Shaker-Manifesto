Deaths. Tabitha A. Hardin Tabitha A. Hardin, at Pleasant Hill, Ky. Jan. 24, 1899. Age 78 years. Sister Tabitha has been a member of this society for more than sixty years. Of her we can say, 'She hath done what she could.' J. W. S. Zelinda Smith Zelinda Smith, at Enfield, 'NT. H. Jan. 30, 1899. Age 79 years. It was the birthday of Sister Zelinda, who had been feeble for several months.
