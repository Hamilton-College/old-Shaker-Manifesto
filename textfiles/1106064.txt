Good advice is given Congress by the Philadelphia Times, which says the members had better pray during the session and thus save the country much trouble. Most of them probably know as little of prayer as of national matters, and it would be necessary to appoint a clergyman speaker to keep them in order. It is said that Ben Hill bet Alexander Stephens five dollars the latter could not say the Lord's prayer. When Stephens took the bet and began, ' Now I lay me down to sleep,' Hill said, 'Here's your money Stephens, I've lost.
