Deaths Catharine L. Hall Catharine L. Hall at Harvard, Mass. Dec. 1, 1890. Age 77 yrs. and 2 mo. Sister Catharine has been a resident of the Society since childhood, and was much beloved. Her's was a life of constant devotion to the cause she so nobly espoused. Peace be to her memory. C. B. W. Rhoda Gray Rhoda Gray at White Water, O. Nov. 8, 1890. Age 67 yrs., 8 mo. and 11 days.
