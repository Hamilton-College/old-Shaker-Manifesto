The Inspiration of the Bible The Inspiration of the Bible, otherwise known as Will the Old Book Stand I'a most widely circulated work; being in its third million, and having consumed over seventy tons of paper in its production. It has been translated into nearly twenty languages. The late Lord Shaftesbury declared it to be 'One of the most valuable essays of modern times,' and D. L. Moody has been the means of circulating large quantities. Pub. by H. L. Hastings, 47 Coruhill, Boston, Mass.
