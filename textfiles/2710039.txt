The Outlook In the Educational Number of The Outlook Professor David P. Todd contributes an account of the Amherst Expedition to Japan to view the latest solar eclipse, and also explains in a really fascinating way, aided by pictures, the ingenious methods which science has lately adopted for this kind of astronomical work. This issue of The Outlook has seven richly illustrated articles, besides a charming short story, poems, other contributed articles, and the usual full news editorials and review departments. [$3. a year. The Outlook Co, 13 Astor Place. New York.]
