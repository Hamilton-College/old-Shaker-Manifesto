Types and Scenes from the Midway Plaisance THE MIDWAY WORLD. Types and Scenes from the Midway Plaisance. This is one among- the many beautiful Books that have been given to the world, to hold in remembrance tbe wonderful exhibition that was the glory of Chicago in 1893. It will be issued in 15 parts, 16 pages to a part, and this will be a beautiful half tone work. It will contain 240 illustrations of those race types, their homes and occupations that made the Midway the study and delight of all visitors.
