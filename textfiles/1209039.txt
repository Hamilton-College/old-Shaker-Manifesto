Books and Papers The Household The Household is a Journal devoted to the interests of the American House-wife. Geo. E. Cbowxix, Editor. BrattleboroVt. Its departments are The Veranda, Drawing Room, Conservatory, Nursery, Library, Music, Dispensary, Dressing Room, Dining Room, Kitchen and Parlor. Every Department is abundantly filled with interesting &amp; instructive matter, and cannot be otherwise than an ever present aid to those who are so fortunate as to enjoy its presence.
