Self-defense is natural; but like all good, its abuse becomes evil... Self-defense is natural; but like all good, its abuse becomes evil. We can so encase ourselves in armor of self-righteousness, that neither the advice of friends nor the criticism of foes can affect us. Thus, like a plant, shielded from storm and sunshine, living on its exhalations, we become dwarfed and undeveloped— unfit for either ornament or use. — Amelia Calver
