'Now,' said the teacher in a primary class to one of his pupils, to whom he was trying to impart a knowledge of division, but with little success, 'if you had a pie, and I should ask you for a quarter of it, and you should give me what I wanted, how much would you have left?' 'I wouldn't have any left!' quickly responded the little girl, who seemed to think that the teacher's wants were by no means small.Yonkers Statesman.
