American newspaper annual We have received from N. W. Ayer &amp; Son, Philadelphia, a copy of their American Newspaper Annual for 1884. It is a fine specimen of typography, and is printed on heavy and handsome paper. It contains valuable information and statistic , making it a desirable acquisition to any library, while to the business man and particularly to the advertiser it is invaluable. The price is $3.00 carriage paid.
