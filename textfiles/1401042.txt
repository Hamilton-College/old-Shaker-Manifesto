Herald of Health Herald of Health. Dec. This number contains an extended list of articles on Health which is well worth the perusal by those who love long life and happy days. The drinking of hot water as a remedy for the many diseases that afflict the human family is fully illustrated in the several rules that are given for its use. The department which contains 'Studies in Hygiene for women' has also articles of real value. The work for 1884 will be enlarged by eight additional pages. M. L. Holbrook, M. D.
