HOME TOPICS Indian Chemical Soap To ten lbs. soft water, add two qts. of new rum, fourteen lbs. sal-soda, and you have your soap. Said to be good for Tan, Freck- les, &amp;c. After application wash in soft water. A good chance for the rum-seller to dispose of his rum profitably. Cement for Wood and Stone Take two parts ashes, three parts clay, and one of sand; this composition mixed with Linseed Oil, resists the weather, and is as hard as marble.
