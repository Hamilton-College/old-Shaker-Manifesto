Canterbury and Enfield, New Hampshire... Canterbury and Enfield, N. H., have been having a wonderful flow of the milk of maple. Of this they make a superior syrup, genuine, unadulterated,eleven pounds to the gallon. Friends, do you want some? Send orders to Henry Cummings, Enfield, N. H. $1.25 per gallon.
