Eldress Betsey Smith Eldress Betsey Smith, at South Union, Ky. Feb. 21, 1897. Age 83 years 6 mo. and 25 days. Eldress Betsey entered the Community when ten years of age. She has been a faithful supporter of the principles of our faith, and was greatly beloved by all. During forty-five years she officiated in the order of Ministry. J. C.
