I herein enclose 6Octs. for the good 'Manifesto'... Wau pacca, Wis. I herein enclose 6Octs. for the good 'Manifesto' for another year. I am pleased with it. It is eagerly read each month. I wish every family in the land would take a copy for the spirit of its benign and truth loving pages would be a powerful leaven, working for good in the hearts of our poor, suffering race.
