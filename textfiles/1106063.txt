A hog, an abomination to a Mohammedan, walked into the open door of a mosque one day, and ran all about the building before he could be driven out. The temple was horribly defiled to the minds of the faithful. What could be done 1 The priest explained that the mosque was so holy that the hog became a lamb all the time he was in, but a hog again when he went out. This theory satisfied all. Those priests are about as ingenious as some of our modern preachers, who think the lottery business is all right in a church house, but a terrible sin in any other house.
