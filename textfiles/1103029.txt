A little boy was tempted... A little boy was tempted to pluck some cherries from a tree which his father had forbidden him to touch. ' You need not be afraid,' said his evil companion ; '' for if your father should find out that you have taken them, he is too kind to hurt you.' ' Ah,' said the brave little fellow, ' that is the very reason why I would not touch them, for though my father would not hurt me, yet I should hurt him by my disobedience.'
