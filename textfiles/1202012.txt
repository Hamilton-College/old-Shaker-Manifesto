One of the Shaker Sisters having occasion to visit a City of New England, found herself unprepared for a shower which came while she was passing to the Depot. To protect a nice bonnet, she threw a kerchief over her head which adjusted itself without reference to style. A few moments after this she overtook a company of children on their way to school, who, on seeing her, opened their eyes with astonishment.
