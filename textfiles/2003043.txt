Deaths. William Shippee Wm. Shippee, (late of North Union) at Union Village. Feb. 1, 1890. Age 61 yrs. 1 mo. and 15 days. A good, and upright man. A bright ornament in his character was special solicitude for the friendless. O. C H. Angeline Turner Angeline Turner at Church, Mt. Lebanon, N. Y. Feb. 5, 1890. Age 43 yrs. and 5 days. Thus though from 'Love's shining circle, the gems drop away,' we rejoice to know that our loved one is 'Not lost but gone before.' A. J. C.
