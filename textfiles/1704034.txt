Worcestershire sauce is composed of... Worcestershire sauce is composed of vinegar, allspice, cloves, black pepper, mustard, ginger, salt, shallots, sugar, tamarinds, sherry, curry powder and cayenne pepper. A writer in the Cook, says : ' The design of the man who invented this compound was to furnish a universal table disinfectant by the use of which objectionable food might be rendered tolerable. It extinguishes the native flavor of everything upon which it is used, and makes all taste alike. Only the ignorant mistake it for a true sauce ; it should rank with carbolic acid and chloride of lime as a powerful disinfectant. ' Footers Monthly.
