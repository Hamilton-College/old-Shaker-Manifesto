Books and Papers The Phrenological Journal and Science of Health The Phrenological Journal and Science of Health, for December has portraits of several notable people of to-day, and interesting articles illustrative of their life and character. Thomas C. Mendenhall, the President of the American Association of Science; Mrs. M. E. H. G. Dow, ex-president of a Eailroad in Dover, N. H. George M. Pullman, the originator of the Pullman Palace Car. Many other articles of valuable information may be found in this number. The department of Science of Health has an excellent article on Temperance and Reform and is well worth a careful study. Fowler &amp; Wells Co., 775 Broadway, N. Y.
