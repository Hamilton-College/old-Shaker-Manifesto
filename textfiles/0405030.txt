EDITORIAL WANTS Wanted, for coming number of Shaker and Shakeress, one short, well written, well condensed article, from each Society. By one, we mean one from Brethren and one from Sisters. To secure this, each Family might write one article, as above. The surplus may be placed where we often go for some choice contributions. In the Shaker are one hundred and twenty inches — about six inches to each Society. In the Shakeress, there are ninety inches, being five and a fraction to each. — [Ed.
