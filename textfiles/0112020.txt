The responsibility of receiving and preparing material for our columns... The responsibility of receiving and preparing material for our columns, as well as directing each individual wrapper, rests upon the Resident Editor. The folding and wrapping, ready for mailing, is the gratuitous offering of Sister Harriet Bullard with her able corps of youthful assistants. This is to be appreciated. Bowing in gratitude for past favors, we invite your immediate renewal of subscriptions that we may continue to ' fly abroad ' many years to come. The Shaker
