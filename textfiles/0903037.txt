THOUGHTS FOR TO-DAY No matter how pious men are... No matter how pious men are, the moment they place policy before principle, they become incapable of doing right, and are transformed into the most odious tools of despotism. A man may be in heaven... A man may be in heaven long before he goes there; and a man may be perfectly sure that there is a hell here, even when he is doubtful about the one hereafter.
