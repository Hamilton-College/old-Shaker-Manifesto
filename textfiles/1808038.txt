A CHILD'S EXPLANATION OF A TEXT. Christ said, 'If any man abide not in me, he is cast forth as a branch and is withered and men gather them and cast them into the fire and they are burned.' In the spring the dead branches of trees and vines are gathered and they are burned, and Christ took this illustration in his spiritual work and I understand that if I do not obey Christ's teachings and keep his golden rule, I shall be a little withered branch. G. B.
