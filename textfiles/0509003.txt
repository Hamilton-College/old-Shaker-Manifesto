CORRESPONDENCE Extract of a letter from Wm. Wheeler... Extract of a letter from Wm. Wheeler, dated Hebron, Ind., June 23, 1875, to Elder J. S. Prescott, of North Union, Ohio. ' Please tell me if you believe in the Bible alone as the perfect rule and guide to heaven and eternal life ? And also, will the ' Shaker and Shakeress ' allow our brief correspondence to be inserted in it ? Please let me know. I hope to hear from you again soon. We join in sending our best love and wishes to all. I must say I have particular regards for you and yours. Hoping all for the best, Wm. Wheeler, P. O. Box 38.'
