Deaths. Rufus Crosman Rufus Crosman, at Mount Lebanon, N. Y. Jan. 22, 1891. Age 92 yrs. 5 mo. and 7 days. His 'passing over' seemed only like one going a journey. He laid aside his work, bade his friends farewell and departed. A. J. C. Isaac Bailey Isaac Bailey at Shirley, Mass., Jan. 29, 1891. Age 85 yrs. 1 mo. and 4 days. J. W. Mollie Mcgrew Mollie Mcgrew at Center Family, Union Village, O. Feb. 13, 1891. Age 13 yrs. Sister Mollie has lived in the Community about twelve years. C. C.
