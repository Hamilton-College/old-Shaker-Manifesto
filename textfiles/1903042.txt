Philip Schaff says, in his '-History of the Christian Church, ' Vol. vi, p. 493, ' The Anglo-American theory of the Lord's day which is based on the perpetual essential obligation of the Fourth Commandment, is of Puritan origin at the close of the sixteenth century.' It is this ' theory,' to be enforced by law, to which I understand the 'Sabbath Union' is committed. But it is not accepted by all Christians, is repugnant to the great majority of others, and is therefore impracticable. in Christian standard. B. J. R.
