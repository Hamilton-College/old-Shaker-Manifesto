Indiana Pilkington Indiana Pilkington, at Pleasant Hill, Ky. Dec. 25, 1897. Age 91 years and 7 months. Sister Indiana has been a member of this Society for twenty-nine years. She was a woman of sterling character and of intelligence. All who knew her, loved her and she was, indeed, a Mother in Israel. Death had no terror, and she gladly obeyed the summons, 'Come up higher.' J. W. S.
