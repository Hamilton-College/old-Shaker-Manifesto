DEATHS Prudence Sawyer Prudence Sawyer at North Union, Ohio, Sept. 30. Age, 65 yrs. 4 mo. and 26 days. Betsey Knowles Betsey Knowles , at Harvard, Mass. Oct. 4. Age, 80 yrs. She is one of the few who renounced the world and gave her life and strength to the gospel cause without reserve. So pass our aged friends away.
