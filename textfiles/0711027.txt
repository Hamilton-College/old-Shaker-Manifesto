OBITUARY Ruth A. Green At Shakers, N. Y., Sept. 23, 1877, aged 62 years, Ruth A. Green . Beautiful by nature, and made more so by gospel graces, we sorrowed to part with her. John Holt Also, Oct. 8, John Holt , aged 60. Polly Turney At Shakers, N. Y., Oct. 10, 1877, Polly Turney , aged 64. She turned her back to the world ; lived and died according to her first faith.
