SOCIETY RECORD Shakers, N. Y. Shakers, N. Y. Perhaps, no year has passed, when larger assemblages from strangers and neighbors met with us at our Church on Sundays. With very many who come/Jit cannot be mere curiosity that attracts them, for they are very regular attendants. We have established a stand for the disposal of 'Plain Talks,' 'Brief Exposition,' and copies of The Manifesto at cost. We urge other Societies to do so ; for by it we can circulate our ideas, and thereby defray the cost of a number or two of The Manifesto. Alfred, Me. Considerable moving of buildings, with other improvements at this place, will greet visitors hereafter with pleasant surprise. The gospel lives here also.
