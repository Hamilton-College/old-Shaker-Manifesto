The Shakers believe the kingdom of heaven has been established; that Christ has come upon earth a second time in the person of Mother Ann (Ann Lee, an English woman, born in Manchester, was the founder of the sect, Laving emigrated thence with a small band of disciples shortly before the Revolutionary war), and that the personal reign of God has been restored. They believe, also, that the new dispensation has begun; that, Adam's sin has been atoned; that man has been delivered from all errors except his own ; that the earth and all it contains shall be redeemed. Religio-Philosophical Journal
