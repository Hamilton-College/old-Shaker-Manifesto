Books and Papers. The Phrenological Journal and Science of Health The Phrenological Journal and Science of Health for April has an article from personal observation on Herbert Spencer by J. A. Fowler. Among a long list of good things that is said of the great man is this. 'Herbert Spencer has a grand head, and almost better still, he has been able to use it availably.'
