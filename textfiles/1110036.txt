In the year 1848, James Shackleton, of Lawrence, Mass., found a man in whom he thought there was so little guile that he advised him to join the Shakers! That man so 'jined,' that in 'all the east' no brighter star of Shakerdom shines to-day. We call him our ' beautiful eastern minister.' This good friend, Shackleton, has been an unfailing subscriber to the Manifesto from the start, and now he sends us $5 as a gift!
