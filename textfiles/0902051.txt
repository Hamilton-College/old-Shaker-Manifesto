Bishop Jagger, of the Protestant Episcopal Church, said in a recent sermon : ' The true corrective for wrangling controversies and dangerous extravagances of teaching is the strengthening by every means, by preaching, by teaching, by prayer, by example, of a living, positive faith in the Saviour. This 42 THE SHAKEE MANIFESTO. draws men in toward the center; this softens their asperities of feeling ; this restores the balance of their minds ; this anchors them so firmly in essentials that they cannot be shipwrecked.'
