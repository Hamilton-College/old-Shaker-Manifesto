HEAR MUCH, BUT SAY LITTLE FRANCIS S. SMITH 'Hear much, but say little,' my masters — You'll find it an axiom strong— 'Twill save you from many disasters, As you journey life's pathway along. 'Hear much, but say little,'—you'll find it A wise thing to bridle your speech— There's freedom from trouble behind it— 'Tis better to practice than preach. If you meet with a wise man or scholar, And out in his company walk, Let your mind his grand ideas follow, For you nothing will learn if you talk. N. Y. Weekly.
