Catherine Walker Catherine Walker, at Harvard, Mass. Sept. 10, 1894. Age 75 yrs. 9 mo. and 6 days. She entered the Society in Harvard, in the winter of 1847, and remained as a faithful and interested worker until her death. During a period of over forty years she occupied an official position in which she displayed unwearied energy and faithfulness to duty. She was ever a loyal exponent of the faith and principles upon which this Order is founded. She is much loved, and deeply regretted. M. M.
