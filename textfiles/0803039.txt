HOME TOPICS To brown butter... To Brown Butter : a simple recipe but a useful one. Put a lump of butter, as large as the gravy demands, into a frying pan. When it is melted, dredge it with browned flour, and stir to a smooth batter until it boils. Use this to color and flavor gravies. It makes a nice sauce of itself. Lemon or Orange Butter Take one lemon, grate the rind, add the entire juice ; add two well beaten eggs, and one cup of sugar; mix thoroughly, and use as an accompaniment to bread and butter, or separately as a preserve. For Orange butter, use an orange instead of a lemon.
