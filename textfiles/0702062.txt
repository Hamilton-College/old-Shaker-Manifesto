A Turk once took a piece of white cloth to a dyer to have it dyed black... A Turk once took a piece of white cloth to a dyer to have it dyed black. He was so pleased with the result, that after a time he went to him with a piece of black cloth, and asked to have it made white. But the dyer answered : 'A piece of white cloth is like a man's reputation ; it can be dyed black, but you cannot make it white again.'
