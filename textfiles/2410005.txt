FIRE RECORD. Burning of the Big Barn on the Farm at Harvard. Ayer, Mass. Sept. 20.Fire destroyed the barn of the Harvard Shakers, one of the largest in the State, this afternoon. It contained 125 tons of hay, 450 bushels of potatoes and a large quantity of ensilage. The Ayer Fire Department was sent for and did good work, saving all the stock and large quantities of cord wood. The total loss is $10,000, with no insurance. Boston Journal.
