Deaths. Susannah M. Gooden Susannah M. Gooden, at Harvard, Mass. Apr. 15,1891. Age 95 yrs. 3 mo. and 6 days. South Union, Ky., Apr. 23, 1891. Willie Myers Willie Myers, nearly nine years of age was drowned on the 21st. inst. He went into the pond at the wrong place and his first step carried him into deep water, and he was drowned before any assistance could be rendered. H. L. E.
