Eliza Wells , at Watervliet, N. Y. May 1. Age, 84 years, 9 mo. and 14 days. She was the daughter of Calvin and Betsy Wells, and is the last of the noted family of Wells, that were members of our Society. Her long life of strict adherence to the principles of Christianity, has been marked for its charity, humility and enduring fortitude. Patient under trials and always ready to assist others. She was a dearly beloved and praise worthy sister, and has now passed on to receive the reward of well doing.
