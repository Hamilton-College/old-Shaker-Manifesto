Deaths. William Johnson William Johnson, at Shakers, Albany Co., N. Y. Dec. 18, 1898. Age 59 years and 6 mo. He has been a member of the Community twenty-one years. We mourn the loss, but lie has gone to reap the reward of his faithful labors. J. B. Andrew Bloomberg Andrew Bloomberg, at Pleasant Hill, Ky. Dec. 26, 1898. Age 80 years. Br.
