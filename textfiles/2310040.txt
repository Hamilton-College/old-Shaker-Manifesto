The Corruptions of the New Testament Fraternal Tidings. Among the many very valuable publications that are sent out on a mission into the world, is a little work entitled, 'The Corruptions of the New Testament,' published by H. L. Hastings, of Boston, Mass. No one can read this little volume without being much better informed on the subject of the New Testament writings, and certainly they would converse with a clearer understanding of their subject. Price 35 cts. J. K. Hastings, 47 Cornhill, Boston, Mass.
