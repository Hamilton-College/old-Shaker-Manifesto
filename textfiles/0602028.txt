After the death of Abdermen, calif of Cordova, the following paper was found After the death of Abdermen, calif of Cordova, the following paper was found in his own handwriting: 'Fifty years have elapsed since I became calif. I have possessed riches, honors, pleasures, friends, in short every thing that man can desire in this world. I have reckoned up the days in which I could say that I was really happy, and they amount to fourteen.' Oh, the consummate vanity of worldly fame and greatness!
