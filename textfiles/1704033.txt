An aged and devoted minister was tried very much in his mind... An aged and devoted minister was tried very much in his mind by the persistent use of tobacco by a brother in the church. At last he said to the brother, ' How it would look, my brother, if you was in heaven, for the Lord to send an angel around after you with a mop to clean up the floor. ' The good brother has never 'chewed' since. Lewiston Journal.
