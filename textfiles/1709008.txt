My good sir: Will you kindly... Exeter, N. H., June 23, 1887. My good sir :- -Will you kindly give me information regarding the Shakers? Their history, customs, religious services, requirements for membership. Do they perform the ceremony of baptism? If so, by immersion or sprinkling? Have they a creed or ^articles of faith? A reply will greatly oblige, most sincerely yours, C. M.
