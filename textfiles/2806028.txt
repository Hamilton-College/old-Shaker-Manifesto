Irena George Irena George, at Enfield, N. H. May 22, 1898. Age 91 years 1 mo. and 13 clays. She had been with the Society from a child and had given all the years of active life, to the interests of the consecrated Home, and to the maintenance of those principles which are its foundation. She is gathered home, as a sheaf fully ripe for the Harvester! R. C.
