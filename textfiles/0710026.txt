Elder Evans preached before an immense concourse... Elder Evans preached before an immense concourse of intelligence at Shakers, N. Y., Sept. 2. Those present, give him excellent credit. Some of our neighbors—florists— proposed crowning him with a beautiful wreath of immortelles; and they may have done so! Greeley Mohawk Grapes, ripened here this year, August 25, ten days later than usual. Beautiful flavor, large clusters, and no dropping from the stem. Trustees of Watervliet Society (Shakers, N. Y.), at present: Chauncy Copley, Philip Smith, Henry George, Ruth A. Green, Samantha Borvie, Adelaide Ingham. Erratum.—In September number, page 66, first paragraph of second column, read 'the priesthood was dual,' instead of 'dead.' The meaning was thereby destroyed.
