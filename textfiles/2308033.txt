The New England Magazine SPECIAL LITERARY NOTE. The New England Magazine with the current issue for July passes into the hands of Warren F. Kellogg, who has purchased the assets of the old company, and will continue the publication of the magazine, managing it himself from its new offices at 5 Park Square, Boston. Mr. Kellogg was formerly Treasurer of the Boston Post, under the old regime, when it stood for all that was highest in American journalism, and more recently he has been interested in different Boston magazines. Edwin D. Mead, the chief editor of the New England, under the old stock company, and Walter Blackburn Harte, the managing editor, will be associated with Mr. Kellogg in his new enterprise.
