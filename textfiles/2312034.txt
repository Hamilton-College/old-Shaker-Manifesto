Deaths. Sally Simpson Sally Simpson, at Alfred, Me. Sept. 28, 1893. Age 77 years and 25 days. A faithful member of this Society for fifty-eight years. F. C. Hannah Freeheart Hannah Freeheart, at South Union, Ky. Oct. 23, 1893. Age 90 years. She has been in this Society since its organization. She was faithful in every duty. J. H. E.
