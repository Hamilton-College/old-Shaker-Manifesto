Eunice Bathrick Harvard, Mass. July, 1883. Our Beloved and venerable sister, Eunice Bathrick, migrated to the next phase of human existence, June 24th, in the 90th, year of ler age. She was the oldest proprietor in he Society, and was widely known b'y her etters of correspondence with the different Societies, and many other friends who will miss the inspiiation of her encouraging epistles. Her's is a Crown of life. Elijah Myrick.
