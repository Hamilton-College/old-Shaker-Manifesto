Eldress Angeline Alvira Conklin Eldress Angeline Alvira Conklin, at Shakers, N. Y. June 4, 1898. Age 72 years 4 mo. and 10 days. She has been among Believers since childhood, has filled all places of care and trust in the family. Faithfully, honestly and conscientiously she has proved her faith by her works. 'No greater love and consecration can any soul show, than to willingly lay down his life for the cause.' This she has done. In her we lose a mother, counselor and friend. I. A.
