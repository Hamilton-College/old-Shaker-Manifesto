Deaths. Charles Johnson Charles Johnson at South Union, Ky. June 8, 1897. Age 65 years. Br. Charles has been in the Community 18 years. He was an industrious and excellent workman. J. W. P. Elbridge Gerry Elbridge Gerry at Soutli Union, Ky. July 10, 1897. Age 63 years. He entered the Community when a child and became an honored member of the Society. For thirty years he officiated as a Family Deacon and was loving and conscientious in the discharge of all his duties. J. W. P.
