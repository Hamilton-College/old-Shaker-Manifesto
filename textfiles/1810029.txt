Deaths. Susan Ann Miner Susan Ann Miner, at North Union, Ohio, August 5, 1888. Aged 64 yrs. &amp; 4 months. She kept the gospel from early youth, and was a peacemaker. S. S. Miner. Laura Russell Laura Russell, at North Union, Ohio, August 7, 1888. Aged 86 yrs. &amp; 6 months. She was a kind, loving sister. S. S. Miner.
