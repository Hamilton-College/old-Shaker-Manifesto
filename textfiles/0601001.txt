HENRY VINCENT ON ELDER EVANS Henry Vincent tells us this story: '' While Elder Frederic W. Evans was holding meetings in England in 1871, one of the Christian brethren became somewhat nervously excited over some of the theological views expressed by the good elder, and called out: ' What did Jesus Christ come into this world for ? I demand of you what Jesus Christ came into this world for ?' Elder Frederic paused in his discourse, and slightly leaning forward to get a better view of his assailant, spoke in his peculiar, clear voice: ' My friend, Jesus Christ came into this world to teach such men as you are how to govern their tempers.'
