THE MANIFESTO. JANUARY, 1898. OFFICE OF PUBLICATION. The Manifesto is publisht by the 'United Society of Believees' on the first of each month, and is the only work issued regularly by the Shakee Community. Its aim is to furnish a plain and simple statement of the religious views of the Obdee and to inculcate the spirit of righteousness. Address all communications to Heney C. Blinn, East Canterbury, Mer. Co., N. H. TEHMS. .50 One copy one year, postage paid. A cross in the margin will show that your subscription has closed. Remittances for subscription by Money Order should be on the Post Office at Concord, N. H.
