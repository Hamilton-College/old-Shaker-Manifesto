Deaths. Aurelia White Aurelia White at Watervliet, N. Y., May 13, 1889. Age 71 yrs. and 3 mo. Sister Aurelia was loved and respected for her unflinching devotion to the Cause which she espoused in her childhood. She gave her whole life in sustaining the Principles of righteousness. Her work is finished and she has gone to join with kindred souls in the beautiful land, beyond. J. B.
