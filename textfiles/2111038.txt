Palmer Cox has signed a contract with The Ladies' Home Journal... Palmer Cox has signed a contract with The Ladies' Home Journal whereby his amusing little 'Brownies,' which he has made so marvelously successful in St. Nicholas and in his books, will hereafter belong exclusively to the Journal. Mr. Cox's contract begins with the October number, for which he has drawn the first of an entirely new series of adventures of his 'funniest little men in the world.'
