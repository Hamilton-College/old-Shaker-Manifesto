The Little Christian The Little Christian is a beautifully illustrated paper for children, and is filled with choice reading, eminently calculated to amuse, instruct and to form a basis for future usefulness. The little folks are fortunate to obtain so much that is calculated to give them pleasure for so trifling a sum. One copy one year, (two numbers each month,) 25 cts. II. L. Hastings, 47 Cornhill, Boston, Mass.
