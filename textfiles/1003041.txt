The slippery season has worked terribly with some of the saints here. Received from Benj. H. Smith, $12.00. Enfield, N. H. That was quite a severe loss of the North Family, to have a val uable team of horses disappear beneath the ice of their beautiful lake. We sympathize. Harvard, Mass.
