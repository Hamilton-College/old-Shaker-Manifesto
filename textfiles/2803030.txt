Deaths. Melinda Hubbard Melinda Hubbard, at Enfield, N. H. Jan. 23, 1898. Age 78 years 5 mo. and 14 days. She had been with the Society for sixty years and was widely known and universally loved and respected both by those of her own home and many who had shared her loving ministrations outside the home circle. Such, we know hear the welcome summons, 'Well done, good and faithful servant, enter thou into the joy of thy Lord. R. C. Hannah P. Shepard Hannah P. Shepard, at East Canterbury N. H. Feb. 1, 1898. Age 56 years, 11 mo. and 20 days.
