One of the teachers ask?d her scholars how they liked the sea. 'Very much, miss,' replied a child; 'but where are the tinnamies?' 'The tinnamies, my child! What do you mean ?' 'Why, you know,' the child replied again, 'the tinnamies that go with the sea. You know the Commandment says, 'the sea and all the tinnamies.' ' This was the way the child had been repeating 'the sea and all that in them is.'
