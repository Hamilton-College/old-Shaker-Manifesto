Merrill Bailey Merrill Bailey at Alfred, Me., April 4, 1893. Age 92 yrs. 7 mo. and 7 days. He was born in Colebrook, N. H., and came to this Society with Ms father, Moses Bailey, when but twelve years of agel During these eighty years Br. Merrill has occupied from time to time nearly every position of burden and trust in the Society and always with fidelity and honor. By all who knew him he will be remembered as 'that noblest work of God, an honest man.' J. B. V.
