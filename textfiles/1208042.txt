Shaker Brown Bread. Mix together 2 qts. Indian meal, 1 qt. rye meal, and 1 pt. canaille (coarse flour.) Take 1 pt. of this mixed meal, add water and scald it thoroughly, and when cool, add a teaspoonful of stock yeast and let stand to rise. To the remainder of the meal, add 3 qts. sweet milk, scalding hot. Mix thoroughly with pudding stick, and when cooled to lukewarm, add the yeasted portion. Put in the oven immediately, and bake with a steady heat for seven or eight hours.
