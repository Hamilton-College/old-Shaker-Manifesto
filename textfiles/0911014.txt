'It is a pity that a good share of the indignation visited upon wrong thinking might not be bestowed upon wrong living. A little more emphasis in the life is what we Want. Orthodox doctrine without orthodox living is the shell of the cocoanut with the meat gone — the frame of a house having neither clapboard nor shingle ; and yet just this some people make their refuge, and then wonder when the storms do come that theyare not sheltered.—Christian at Work.
