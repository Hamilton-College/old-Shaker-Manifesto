THE MANIFESTO. DECEMBER, 1895. OFFICE OF PUBLICATION. The Manifesto is published by the 'United Society op Believers' on the first of each month, and is the only work issued regularly by the Siiakeb Community. Its aim is to furnish a plain and simple statement of the religious views of the Okdee and to inculcate the spirit of righteousness. All communications should be addressed to Henry C.
