Daniel Fraser Daniel Fraser at the North Family, Mt. Lebanon, N. Y. Oct. 10, 1889. Age 85 yrs. and 5 mos. He has spent fifty-five years in devotion to the cause of Zion, and was a zealous laborer for the good of humanity through all his long life. D. O. When brother Daniel's hand refused to wield the pen, he thus sang from his heart. 'Come, 0 Death, thou great uplifter, Touch me with thy genial rod; Sever earthly ties asunder, Lay my body 'neath the sod. Then in spirit I shall triumph In the city of my God.'
