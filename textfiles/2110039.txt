Deaths. Alexander Morrison Alexander Morrison, at Mt Lebanon, N. Y.. May 31, 1891. Age 38 yrs. 10 mo. and 11 days. He was a Scotchman, and had lived in this Society about two years. O. H. William Davidson William Davidson at Harvard, Mass. Aug. 22, 1891. Age 77 years. Br. Wm. entered the Community when a young man and has been an honorable member. He has proved through his whole life that he desired a full salvation. A. D. B.
