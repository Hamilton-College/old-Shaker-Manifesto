THE MANIFESTO. DECEMBER, 1897. OFFICE OF PUBLICATION. The Manifesto is publisht by the 'United Society of Believers' on the first of each month, and is the only work issued regularly by the Shakeu Community. Its aim is to furnish a plain and simple statement of the religious views of the Okder and to inculcate the spirit of righteousness. Address all communications to Henky C.
