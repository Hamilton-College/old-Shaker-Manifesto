PRAYER Canterbury, N. H. 1. O, Father, to Thy throne we come, In attitude of prayer, 2. O, bless us with a fervent zeal, To know and do thy will; Our hearts petitioning Thy grace, Thy With more abundant righteousness Our care, fill: We That ask for pow er to control The elements of earth; we may walk with purpose fix'd The path way of the pure ; For wisdom, to expand the soul Unto the higher birth. Fulfilling all the law of grace, Thy favor to insure. guidance, love, and under standings
