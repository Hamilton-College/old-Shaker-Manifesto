Deaths Lydia Lewis Lydia Lewis at South Family, Mt. Lebanon, Feb. 20, 1888. Age 83 yrs. 7 mo. and 20 days. Sister Lydia lived with the order of Elders for thirty years. S. A. C. Minerva Wright Minerva Wright at Union Village, Ohio, Feb. 18, 1888. Age 89 yrs. 11 mo. and 15 days. What unspeakable treasures are these aged Brethren and Sisters. O. C. H.
