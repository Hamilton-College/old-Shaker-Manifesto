The Souvenir The Souvenir comes from the Scarritt, Coif legiate Institute, at Neosho, Mo. The whole of the Souvenir is a treasure-house of good gifts, and many carry an appreciative influence for the prosperity of humanity. Moral worth stands in advance of earthly pleasures or Measures. While an interest is being taken in the lands and climate of Florida, it may he interesting to read of the Koreshans who have purchased a large tract of land in Lee, Co., which is about 26 deg. north latitude.
