The five points—not of Calvinism... The five points—not of Calvinism—but of Ritualism, are 'eastward position, altar lights, vestments, wafer bread and mixed chalice. ' Paul does not mention them, nor yet Peter, nor John. If to-morrow they all vanish from the earth the gospel of Jesus Christ would be as complete, as effectual, and as gracious as it ever was. —Evangelical Churchman.
