SUPPLICATION Enfield, N. H. Hear us, Hear us O righteous God, Hear us, Hear us; Hear our supplication. In humility Hear us, Hear us; we come into Thy house of prayer. Gire t.s strength, O Lord, we pray, To renew our lives to Thee. Open Thy avenues of boundless love, Thy fountains of life-giving waters. Bless Bless us O Lord, us. O Lord, Bless us with Thy blessing. Cover us as with a mantle, from all harm. Bless us and shield us Bring us nigh unto Thee, as children of Thy tender care, and lead us, O God, forever more.
