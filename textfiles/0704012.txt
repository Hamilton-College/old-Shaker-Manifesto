Listening to evil reports The longer I live, the more I feel the importance of adhering to the rule which I have laid down for myself in relation to such matters : i. To hear as little as possible whatever is to the prejudice of others. 2. To believe nothing of the kind till I am absolutely forced to it. 3. Never to drink into the spirit of one who circulates an evil report. 4 Always to moderate, as far as I can, the unkindness which is expressed towards others. 5. Always to believe that, i\ the other side were heard, a very different account would be given of the matter. Camus's Life of Simeon
