A minister startled his audience... A minister startled his audience by saying, 'I have forgotten my notes, and shall have to trust to Providence, but this evening I will come better prepared.' At a revival in a small town the invitation was given for any one who wished to live a better life and desired the prayers of the congregation to rise. A man arose. The congregation was jubilant and noisy with 'amens.' Looking about him he said: 'There is no need of making such a fuss and noise. I ain't the worst man that ever lived.'
