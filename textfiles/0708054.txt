If your room needs deodorizing... If your room needs deodorizing, or a change of air seems very desirable therein, throw open the windows for a few moments, and burn on the stove-hearth, or over the lamp-chimney on some metal substance, a lump of sugar as large as a hickory-nut. Almost any one unfamiliar with this method, will be most agreeably suprised at the result.
