We cannot reach men's hearts till... We cannot reach men's hearts till we can reach their ears. When Jesus uttered that wonderful cry: ' If any man thirst, let him come unto me and drink,' it was not in the presence of the few that believed in him, but in the great Jewish temple, and on the greatest day of the feast, when the largest concourse was assembled. We also should seek to get the attention of all the people we can. They will not come to us ; therefore we must go to them. If we cannot reach them in one way, we should try another, and another.
