A Word to the Careless. —''Order is Heaven's first law.' We should think that those who visit this place, did not expect nor intend to keep Heaven's first law, in this world The neglect to return the articles to their proper places, gives the room a very disorderly appearance. Possibly, they may think that time is too short to allow them to attend? to duty, but that, in eternity, there will be nothing to hinder them from keeping the good old law, and which may, in that day, be as easy to regard, as it is for water to run down, hill.—
