Golden age Golden Opinion of ' The Golden Age.'—Elder Evans, in a late letter to this office, says : '• Of all the newspapers I have seen, The Golden Age has my largest esteem. Its columns fairly overflow with exuberant truth. Among its contributors may be observed men and women who are thoroughly in earnest after truth—a class of reformers who know what truth is, and are determined to find it in its fullness. It deserves success, and I believe will' enjoy it.''
