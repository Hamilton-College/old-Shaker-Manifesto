A Shakeress, living in the United Society of Believers at Mt. Lebanon, N. Y.... A Shakeress, living in the United Society of Believers at Mt. Lebanon, N. Y., and known as 'Aunt Dolly Saxton,' has attained the remarkable age of 104 years. She was born in Columbia county, of American parents, on the 6th of May, 1776, fifty-nine days before the Declaration of American Independence. Her parents were converted to the doctrine of Mother Ann Lee, and joined her church when their child was five years old, and she has always remained an honored member of the order. Brethren's Advocate.
