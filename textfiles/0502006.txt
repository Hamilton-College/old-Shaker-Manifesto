The true social desideratum at last is, not that women, equally with men, assume the exercise of authority ; but that men, equally with women forego the exercise of authority The genuine perfection of humanity instead of being the enforced obedience of one-half to the other half, is the spontaneous obedience of both halves to the law of God. Mill.
