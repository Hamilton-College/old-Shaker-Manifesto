NOT QUESTIONABLE. Dr. Lightfoot says that Adam was created on Friday morning at 9 o'clock, that he ate the fruit about 1 P. M. and that Christ was promised at 3 P. M. Julius Africanus insists that the world was made Sept. 1st., and was 5508 yrs. 3 mo. and 25 days old at the birth of Jesus. He wrote A. D. 220.
