Harriet Burgess Harriet Burgess , at Second Family, West Pittsfield, Mass. Nov. 21, 1891. Age 80 yrs. 2 mo. and 6 days. Sister Harriet came into the Society when fourteen years of age. In the soft twilight of morning Her freed spirit passed away To behold the glorious dawning Of a bright, eternal day. J. L. S.
