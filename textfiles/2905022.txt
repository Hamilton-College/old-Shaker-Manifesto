Deaths. Caroline Taylor Caroline Taylor at Pleasant Hill, Ky. Feb. 25, 1899. Age 63 years. Sister Caroline entered the Society when a child, and has been a kind and worthy Sister. Peace to her memory. J. W. S. Maria Pilot Maria Pilot, at Watervliet, O. April 6, 1899. Age 81 years and 24 days. J. O. T.
