Watervliet, N. Y. Watervliet, N. Y.: It is ever pleasant to record a free use of paint. The Church Family of this place looks almost new, some seven or eight large buildings having been re-covered with paint. An external observer remarked : ' They are making Zion shine 1' Jesse Wells, nearly a hundred years old, the last of the famous family of that name, is the oldest man in Albany county.
