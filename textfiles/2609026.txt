Deaths. Judith Dillon Judith Dillon, at South Union, Ky. July 8, 1896. Age 93 years, 5 mo. and 26 days. Sister Judith is the last of the children of the first settlers in this Society. She has spent eighty-six years here, during which time, she held several offices of trust, and was respected by all who knew her. J. C. Emily Offord Emily Offord, at Canaan, N. Y. July 30, 1896. Age 48 years and 8 months.
