GOING TO SCHOOL. As I walk to school I will describe what I see, when I get started on the side walk, first there are some Willows, Poplars, and Maple are all the way down to school on the left side are some Lilac bushes, Ilackmetack, Crab apple tree, Ash, Apple trees, Horse Chestnut, Pine and fir trees, when I get past two white houses on the left hand side, there is a field with a large quantity of grass and clover, next to that is a smaller place with a fencing around it and. has Pine, Fir and Spruce, and Maple trees some violets grow there, next are rose bushes and the school house itself. J. L. W.
