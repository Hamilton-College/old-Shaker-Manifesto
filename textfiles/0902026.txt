A safe way is, to keep the ashes in an iron can or holder, until cool, and then throw them into the box or receptacle provide d for them. Wood ashes are too valuable to waste, and coal ashes make an excellent absorbent in the earth-closet, and are very good for making hard foot paths. In stormy weather... In Stormy Weather the stock are better under cover than exposed to the wintry blasts. It is a mistake to suppose that exposure makes them hardy; on the contrary, they are weakened by it.
