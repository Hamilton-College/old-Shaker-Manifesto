Deaths Nathan C. Prouty Nathan C. Prouty, at Shirley, Mass. Nov. 21, 1896. Age 70 years, 5 mo. and 11 days. He has lived in the Community from a child. Br. Nathan went to Ayer twice on the 21st and the next morning was found dead. The Physician who was acquianted with Br. Nathan, certified that it was a case of heart failure. J. W.
