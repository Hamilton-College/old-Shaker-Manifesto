Deaths. James S. Kaime James S. Kaime at East Canterbury, N. H. Jan. 3, 1894. Age 73 yrs. G mo. and 14 days. Martha W. Billington Martha W. Billington at Second Family, Mt. Lebanon, N. Y. Jan. 2, 1894. Age 79 yrs. 6 mo. and 6 days. Borne away on death's stern billows, From the surging sea of time, To a land of joy and beauty. Heavenly home in yonder clime; Golden harps are sounding welcome, Vocal tones the strains prolong, 'Tis one grand and glorious concert, Rendered by the angel throng. J. E. S.
