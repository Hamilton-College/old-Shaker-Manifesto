'SECOND OR THIRD?' Elihu Burritt has lately written a very sensible article in The Christian at Work in which he testifies that ' I think we have a hundred fold stronger evidence of the second advent, than we have had of the first.' Such utterances from such notables, are destined to awaken investigation, that may result in affixing the date of the second arrival in 1770 ! Let us all keep our eyes and ears open.
