Gladstone is credited with the following remarks; 'Talk about the questions of the day; there is but one question and that is the Gospel. It can and will correct everything needing correction. All men at the head of great movements are Christian men. During the many years I was in the Cabinet, I was brought into association with sixty master minds, all but five of them were Christians. My only hope for the world is in bringing the human mind into contact with the divine revelation.' Exchange.
