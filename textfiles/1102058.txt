A waterproof whitewash may be made as follows: ' Take half a bushel of lime, slack it with boiling water, and cover to keep in the steam-, strain and add seven pounds of salt dissolved in hot water; three pounds of ground rice, boiled to a paste, and added hot; half pound Spanish whiting, and one pound clean glue, previously dissolved to a thin paste; add five gallons hot water, and stir the whole; cover it, and keep it a few days. It must be used hot. One pint covers a square yard.'
