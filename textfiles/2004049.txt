Clarissa Rankin Sister Clarissa Rankin (the last of the name here,) left the shores of time at 4 A. M. to-day, aged 84 yrs. and 22 days. She was the grand-daughter of Jno. Rankin, the foremost preacher of the noted Kentucky Revival at the beginning of this century. Her father and mother united with Believers here on the 18th. of November, 1807. Clarissa has been a true consecrated soul throughout her long life. 'Blessed are the dead who die in the Lord.' H. L. E.
