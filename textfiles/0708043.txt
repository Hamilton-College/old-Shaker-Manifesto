What posterity will think. 'Our enlightened posterity will look back upon us who eat oxen and sheep just as we look back upon cannibals.' So says the late Winwood Reade, in his essay on 'The Future of the Human Race.' There are several other practices they will perhaps look back upon with a slight disgust. One is war, one intemperance, and one licentiousness. Hurry up, oh, good time coming! if you want us to enjoy jour delightful society. Herald of Health.
