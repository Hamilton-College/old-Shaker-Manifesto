SWEET PRAISE. Enfield, N. H. In songs of sweet praise our voices we will raise, Imploring the angels to aid; Behold! they're descending, in our music are blending, Bright vision of beauty! Each soul, strike the chord. We'll sing away sadness, bring joy and gladness; Sing away trials that cumber the mind; Sing away sorrow, bring hope for the morrow ; Sing away darkness, bring sight to the blind
