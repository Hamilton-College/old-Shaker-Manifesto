Deaths Nathan C. Prouty Nathan C. Prouty, at Shirley, Mass. Nov. 21, 1896. Age 70 years, 5 mo. and 11 days. He has lived in the Community from a child. Br. Nathan went to Ayer twice on the 21st and the next morning was found dead. The Physician who was acquianted with Br. Nathan, certified that it was a case of heart failure. J. W. Thomas MacRae Thomas MacRae, at South Union, Ky. Dec. 27, 1896. Age 65 years. Br. Thomas has lived in the Community some thirty years. He was greatly beloved, and especially by the children. He had charge of the school and was an assistant in the office. J. W. P.
