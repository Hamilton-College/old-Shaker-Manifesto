It is reported that Dr. Gumming, of London, in a recent lecture, announced to his congregation that he firmly believed that many of his hearers would live to witness Christ's second coming. The rapid fulfilling of prophecy indicates the near approach of the great millennium, and it would be a source of much happiness if we could be permitted to realize that the grand event shall take place during the present generation.
