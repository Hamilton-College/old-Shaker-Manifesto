The Progressive Thinker The Progressive Thinker, a new paper in the interest of Spiritualism, edited by J. R. Frances, of Chicago, has entered upon its pilgrimage, and already reached its seventh number of Vol 1. Its 'battle-ax, already, begins to strike and like the 'Lion Heart' may hew its way to a Progressive spirit land. It is pub. monthly at $1. per. year. 251 S. Jefferson St, Chicago, 111.
