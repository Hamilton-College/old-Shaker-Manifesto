Sarah Van Vick Sarah Van Vick, March 29, at Mt. Lebanon, N. Y. Age, 62 yrs. G mo. and 28 days. A faithful burden bearer. Never flinching in the hour of trial, whether her path was among flowers or thorns. Her life has been well spent and from her earthly labors she has gone where 'the wicked cease from troubling and the weary are at rest.' She was dearly beloved by all her Brethren and Sisters. P. C. L.
