Mastery A beautiful Magazine is the No. 12.'Mastery,' filled with Illustrated articles of 'Useful Pastimes for Young People.' If this is a representative number of those published and those to be published, no one can regret its introduction into the family. It has an interesting, useful and healthy tone and must be appreciated by those who are so fortunate as to receive it. Pub. at 842 Broadway, N. Y. $3. a year.
