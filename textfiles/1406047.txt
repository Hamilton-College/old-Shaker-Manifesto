Elhannan Scott Elhannan Scott, Nov. 1, 1883, at Pleasant Hill, Ky. Age 78 yrs. 9 mo and 4 days. He expired suddenly without uttering a word, while attempting to reach a building that was on fire. He was a Trustee in the Church family at the time of his death, and was a very faithful man. He united with the order of Believers at the age of 16 yrs. B. B. D.
