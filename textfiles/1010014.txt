Lomas: Dear Editor. — For some reason unassignable by me, the music in the last issue of the 'Manifesto' is defective in its transcription. The four upper staves containing the simple melody or solo — all of which should have been single—are coupled by two braces, which would greatly bewilder both reader and player, though they might puzzle it out. Would you deem it of enough importance to insert an explanatory remark upon the point in the next issue ? If so, by so doing you would greatly oblige your friend and brother, James G. Russell
