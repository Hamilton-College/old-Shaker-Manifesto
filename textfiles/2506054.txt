SPELLING KITTEN. A dear little girl, With her brain in a whirl, Was asked the word 'kitten' to spell. 'K-double i-t T-e-n,' said she; And thought she had done very well. 'Has kitten two I's ?' And the teacher's surprise With mirth and patience was blent. 'My kitten has two,' Said Marjory Lou; And she looked as she feltquite content. Ladies' Companion.
