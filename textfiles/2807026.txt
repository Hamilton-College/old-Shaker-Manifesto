Deaths. Rebecca Edwards Rebecca Edwards, at South Union, Ky. April 11, 1898. Age 79 years 1 mo. and 25 days. Sister Rebecca entered the Society forty- nine years ago, and has been an interested laborer in the vineyard of the Lord, giving her talents unreservedly to his service. J. C. Sally Ceeley Sally Ceeley, at East Canterbury, N. H. June 4, 1898. Age 92 years 5 mo. and 5 days. A ripened sheaf is gathered into the 'harvest home.' J. E.
