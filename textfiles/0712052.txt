Why the rich are miserable WHY THE RICH ARE MISERABLE. 1 In his speech at the recent award of the Montyon ' prize for virtue, M. Alexander Dumas discussed the : reason why rich people are miserable, and came to i the conclusion that it is because they do not sufficiently devote their wealth to making others happy. One of the passages runs thus: 'The kingdom she has long inhabited is no longer of this world; it knows no limits or frontiers, no foreigners or enemies, no victors or vanquished; all who inhabit it are the children of one father, whose name is Charity.'
