Deaths. Charles J. Preter Charles J. Preter, at Union Tillage, O. Jan. 17, 1892. Age 80 yrs, and 10 mo. Br. Charles has been a faithful member of Society for some 54 years. He was born in Nassau Germany. C. C. Charlotte Leecth Charlotte Leecth at South Union, Ky. Jan. 23, 1892. Age 90 yrs. 8 mo. and 1 day.
