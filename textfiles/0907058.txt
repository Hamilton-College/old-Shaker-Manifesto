Coal Oil and Insects J. A. I discovered several years ago, if charcoal was soaked in coal oil, and a piece as large as a hickory nut was placed beside plants liable to be destroyed by worms, bugs or other insect pests, security was insured aganst their ravages so long as the odor of the oil was given off. I report this for what it is worth. , Atlanta, Ga. [Would not this practice banish vermin from hens' nests ?]
