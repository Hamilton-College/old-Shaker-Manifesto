To agricultural journals To Agricutural Journals. — The very plausible recommendation to plant a few kernels of flaxseed with potatoes to repel the potato bugs is proved to be a cruel hoax. We tested it thoroughly, and the bugs seemed to thrive on it—lay their eggs upon its 'leaves and stalks—and our land is too fully seeded with 'plants out of place' — weeds—flax by name. We hope all Journals will correct the erroneous advice.
