Selections Principle, like truth... Principle, like truth, needs no contrivance, it will tell its own tale and tell it in the same way. It is error only... It is error only, and not truth that shrinks from inquiry. None can look forward to a certainty of future happiness... None can look forward to a certainty of future happiness, unless they are striving to assimilate themselves to the likeness of Him, who was the embodiment of all that is lovely and of good report.
