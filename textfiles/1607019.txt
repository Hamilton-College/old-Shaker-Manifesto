Manifesto has handed to us... 'The Manifesto was handed to us last week by one of our teachers. It opens with the 'Testimony of Itaehel Spencer. ' It contains also many truthful and scientific productions and two strikingly beautiful essays on ' Silence' and ' Inward Heart Throbs.' 'We gladly welcome all such exchanges. ' The above is from the ' Judson Echoes ' published by the Fidelian Society of Judson Female Institute at Marion, Ala. Edited by Miss Mary J. Wyatt.
