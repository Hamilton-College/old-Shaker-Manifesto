The Journal of Hygeio-Therapy The Journal of Hygeio-Therapy for January is very interesting. Dr. Gifford has an article on the use of salt and contends that its use is not a necessity. He then extends his article and treats of the cause of Dropsy and then of the treatment. Prof. Bland has an article on A Big Bacteriological Blunder, which has diverted the attention of physiologists from the correct line of investigation. Prof. Allison says, 'You rarely find one who believes what he eats has any influence on his character.' 'Faith,' says the Bible, 'comes by hearing' and it may come by reading. Read and see. Dr. T. V. Gifford &amp; Co. Kokomo, Ind.
