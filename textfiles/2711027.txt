Maria Foster Maria Foster, at Harvard, Mass. Sept. 9, 1897. Age 75 years 4 mo. and 26 dajs. Sister Maria was born in Concord, Mass., and was placed under the care of the Community when at the age of seven years. When she arrived at the age when she could understanding^ choose for herself, she chose to remain where she had spent her youthful days, therefore becoming a faithful and trusted Christian. She has held many important offices of trust and responsibility. At her death she had been twenty-two years an associate in the min-' isterial order. At one time she had a large class of girls under her charge and taught the public school of the Society for sixteen years. She will be greatly missed by her associates.
