Deaths. Asenath Russell Asenath Russell, at Enfield, N. H. Feb. 28, 1893. Age 40 yrs. 6 mo. and 17 clays. As a lumb without blemish, As a pure spotless dove, Is the soul who has given, All its talents to God. They're like gold in the furnace, Well refined from dross, Who bow in submission To the work of the cross?. M. M. Keniston. Elder Frederick W. Evans Elder Frederick W. Evans, at Mt. Lebanon, N. Y. Mar. 6, 1893. Age 84 yrs 9 mo. and 21 days.
