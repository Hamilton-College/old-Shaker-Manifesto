SHAKERESS On the first of September, the junior Order of Believers at Mt. Lebanon held their annual social gathering in a beautiful pine grove in Canaan. It was a lovely day, replete with social, intellectual and soul enjoyment. We present to the readers of our periodical an interlocutory discourse in verse, written for the occasion by Cecelia Devyr, Ann Offord, Martha J. Anderson, Charlotte Byrdsall, Melissa Soule, Margaret Cleveland, Catharine Allen, and Elvah Collins, and read from memory. We hope it will be accepted.
