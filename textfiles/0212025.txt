Special notice With the beginning of next volume of The Shaker the management changes. The present editor retires to the position of Publisher ; and the present, able head of the Novitiate Orders of Shakers—Elder F. W. Evans— becomes its Editor, and to whom all subscriptions SHOULD BE ADDRESSED. This is a consummation long and devoutly icishedfor. Let the subscriptions be sent to him 'fast and numerous.'
