Deaths. William Heslop William Heslop, at Watervliet. N. Y. Aug. 5, 1888. Age 69 yrs. 4 mo. He united at Watervliet in 1877, and was a faithful and dear Brother, and we shall miss him. S. W. B. Sarah Woodrow Sarah Woodrow, at Center Family, Mt. Lebanon, N. Y. Aug. 6, 1888. Age 81 yrs. 2 mo. and 7 days. She was an interested worker for the gospel cause, and a patient sufferer to the last. E. S.
