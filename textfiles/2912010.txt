A REQUEST By Frederic McKechnie 0 Tuou of truth and grace I am Thy clay. Yet let me see Thy face, I do not pray For riches ; these have wings And flee away. Nor yet Lord would I have A world's acclaim, Worlds go, and with them goes Their thing called fame ; 1 would not have of them Their proudest name.
