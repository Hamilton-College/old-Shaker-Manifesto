Deaths. Susan Ann Miner Susan Ann Miner, at North Union, Ohio, August 5, 1888. Aged 64 yrs. &amp; 4 months. She kept the gospel from early youth, and was a peacemaker. S. S. Miner. Laura Russell Laura Russell, at North Union, Ohio, August 7, 1888. Aged 86 yrs. &amp; 6 months. She was a kind, loving sister. S. S. Miner. Phebe Harris Phebe Harris, at West Family, Pleasant Hill, Ky. April 19, 1888. Age 88 yrs. 4 mo. and 9 days.
