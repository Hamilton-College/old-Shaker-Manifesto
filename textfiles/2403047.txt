Deaths. Rachel Russell Rachel Russell, at Watervliet, Ohio. Jan. 14, 1894. Age 84 yrs. 9 mo. and 20 days. Sister Rachel came to Watervliet with the Brethren and Sisters from North Union. For a long term of years, she was one of the most active and interested members, in bearing the burdens of the Community. A patient and charitable Sister, for whom must come the reward of 'Well Done.' H. W. F. Stanton Buckingham Stanton Buckingham, at Shakers, N. Y. Jan. IT, 1894. Age 92 yrs. 10 mo. and 8 days. Nancy Whitcher Nancy Whitcher, at East Canterbury, N. H. Feb. 1, 1894. Age 88 yrs. 9 mo. and 14 days.
