Deaths. Eleanor Potter Eleanor Potter, at the Church family, Mt. Lebanon, N. Y.. January 21, 1895. Age 82 years, 9 mo. and 21 days. Sister Eleanor has for eighty years been a loved member of our family. Virtuous, intelligent and useful through a long life, when the end came, she 'wrapped the drapery of her couch about her, and lay down to pleasant dreams.' A. J. C. Sarah Small Sarah Small, at South Union, Ky. January 25, 1895. Age 84 years. Eldress Sarah came to South Union at twelve years of age. In early years she laid her life down and devoted her all to the faith she had chosen. At her dying hour she said,'Angels take me, do take me.' She was kind, patient, industrious and pious.
