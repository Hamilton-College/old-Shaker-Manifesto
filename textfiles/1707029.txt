THE TRULY SO. AMELIA J. CALVER. The truly patient suffer the inevitable trials of life without a murmur. The truly unselfish relinquish their own pleasure for others, and serve themselves last. The truly philanthropic will not '' let the right hand know what the left doeth,' and the discriminating point is the needy, not the lovely. The truly good never deviate from principle, for favor or gain. Mt. Lebanon, N. Y.
