Correspondence A SPECIMEN LETTER 'T. C. W. The interest in Shakerism is continually increasing and spreading. The following letter is a specimen of what we are continually receiving. We append the answer : Sir—Having become deeply interested in the Shakers Quebec, 26th, March, 1879. Frederick W. Evans, Mt., Lebanon : ' Sir—Having become deeply interested in the Shakers, both regarding their life and doctrine, and desiring to know more, I would like to obtain a simple plain statement of such, and knowing no other method, write you for it. ' Yours sincerely,' Address Treasury Dep't, Quebec
