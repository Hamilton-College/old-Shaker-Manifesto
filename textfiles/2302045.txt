John Ross John Ross at Mt. Lebanon, N. Y.. Jan. 1, 1893. Age 73 yrs. 9 mo. and 20 days. Br. John was indeed a true and noble Brother. Entering the Community late in life it lias been his sole ambition and determination to be an honor to his home, and creditably bear the name of 'Shaker,' keeping in both letter and spirit, even the smallest rule which governs, guides and protects our Association. A. J. C.
