The Ladies' Home Journal The first and only pictures Mrs. McKinley has had taken in ten years will appear in the October number of The Ladies' Home Journal. They form part of a series of new and unpublisht photographs of the new occupants of the White House. Mrs. McKinley wore for her picture her inaugural ball dress of silver and white brocade, at the special request of the President, who thought it particularly becoming. The excellent portrait of Mr. McKinley in his office was taken in June. The other pictures in the series show the many changes in the arrangements and furnishings of the rooms and grounds, made by the President and Mrs .McKinley.
