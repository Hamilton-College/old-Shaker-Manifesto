The secret of health... The secret of health: First, keep warm. Second, eat regularly and slowly. Third, maintain regular bodily habits. Fourth, take early and very light suppers, or, better still, none at all. Fifth, keep a clean skin. Sixth, get plenty of sleep at night. Seventh, keep cheerful and respectful company. Eighth, keep out of debt. Ninth, don't set your mind on things you don't need.
