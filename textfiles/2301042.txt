MR. BEECHER'S UNPRINTED WORDS MR. BEECHER'S UNPRINTED WORDS. The first of the unpublished intiterial by Henry Ward Beecher, recently purchased by The Ladies' Home Journal, will appear in the January issue of that magazine, and presents among other things, the great preacher's hitherto imprinted opinion on 'Wine' Drinking on New Year's Day.'
