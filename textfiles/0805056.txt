Saltpetre For Lice S. G. Livermore , a practical farmer, states that he has found saltpetre a never-failing preventive of lice. It should be given with salt in the proportion of one pound of saltpetre to a bushel of salt. If you can save the numberless scrubbings with tobacco water and grease and kerosene, &amp;c, &amp;c, it will be an item of no small consequence.
