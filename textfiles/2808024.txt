Ximena Gates Ximena Gates, at Mt. Lebanon, N. Y. June 20, 1898. Age 87 years 2 mo. and 28 days. Our Sister has gone home to rest. Being resurrected in early life into the new and living way, she will join living souls in the eternal spheres and rejoice in the home she has built there by faithfulness and true consecration to gospel principles. J. E. S.
