PURE AIR AND HEARTY FOODS. Those attacked with and suffering from acute diseases should be allowed little or no food. Tanner fasted forty days. A thousand die of gluttony to every one of starvation. Feed the fevers of the young with water only. But not so with consumption or any of the wasting diseases. These require pure, dry ail-, and hearty nourishing, yet easily digested foods.
