The day is certainly not far distant... Professor E. W. Stewart, of Lake View, N. Y. says: 'The day is certainly not far distant when all enterprising farmers will soil their stock to a great extent. The best way to soil is to begin raising grain. Make a calculation as to how much corn and other grain crops you will require, raise good crops and you will soon see the folly of using three to five acres to pasture a cow when she can be soiled on a half acre.
