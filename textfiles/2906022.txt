Laura Dole Laura Dole, at Shakers, N. Y. April 20, 1899. Age 79 years, 3 months and 19 days. Sister Laura came into the Society when a child seven years of age. She has spent a long and useful life among the Believers, and has occupied positions of care and trust. She was a faithful burden-bearer, being for many years a nurse, and afterward, family Deaconess. E. E. W.
