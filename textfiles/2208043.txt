John Sauerborn John Sauerborn, at Watervliet, Ohio. July 6, 1892. Age 68 yrs. 7 mo. and 12 days. Br. John was a faithful man and had been with the Believers some thirty years. He has lived in the order of Elders, and also in the order of Trustees and has always been an honest and upright brother. He has, no doubt, found peace and happiness in his spiritual home. H. W. F.
