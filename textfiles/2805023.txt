Eldress Sophia Copley Eldress Sophia Copley at Enfield, Conn. April 14, 1898. Age 51 years 8 mo. and 17 days. She was born in England and entered the Community in May 1852. Her departure so early, in the midst of a life of usefulness is deeply lamented. She was truly a valiant soldier of the cross of Christ, and altogether a very capable person. In all things ready, faithful and willing. G. W.
