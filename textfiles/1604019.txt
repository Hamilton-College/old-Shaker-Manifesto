BEAUTIFUL LEGEND. Contributed by G. B. A. 'A beautiful vision of our Savior had appeared to a monk, and, in silent bliss he gazed upon it. The hour arrived in which it was his duty to feed the poor of the Convent. He lingered not in his cell to enjoy ihe vision, but left it to perform his humble duty. When he returned he found the humble vision still waiting for him, and uttering these words, ' Hadst thou stayed, I must have fled. '
