Mr. Ruskin at present is advocating dancing as a part of religious education, and thus assails young lady Sunday School teachers: 'At present,' he says, 'you keep the dancing to yourselves, and graciously teach your scholars the catechism. Suppose you were to try for a little while learning the catechism yourselves and teach them to dance.' Albany Express.
