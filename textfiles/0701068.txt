Dr. R. H. Stores is one of the few clergymen... DR. R. H. STORES is one of the few clergymen who refuse' loud calls.' He was invited to Fifth Avenue brick church on the top of Murray hill, at a salary of $10,000, and a millionaire offered to purchase a house which readily sold for $65,000 before the panic and to furnish it handsomely from top to bottom for his use while he remained pastor of the church. and yet he would not ,accept.
