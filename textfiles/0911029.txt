Y., Oct. 11, 1879 My Dear Brother—It is not in our hearts to flatter you nor The Shaker Manifesto, but we think it steadily grows better, and must increase its sphere of usefulness, and 256 THE SHAKER MANIFESTO. be accepted as an encouragement by all seekers after the truth. Believe me, Yours, D. C. Brainard
