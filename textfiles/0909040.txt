The only obstacle now in the way is the dull materiality by which man fetters himself to the earth, his painstaking devotion to stupidity, his self-stultification, his persistent wallowing in the slough of permanent errors, and the resolute sealing of his spiritual capacities to the light which is constantly beaming down upon him. When will he take the same pains to know and to do the will of our Father in heaven, that he now takes to know and do foolishness? and when will he arise and take possession of that heritage which is placed at his disposal through the countless ages of the everlasting?
