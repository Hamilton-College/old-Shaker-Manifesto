Deaths. Elder Nathaniel Fry Elder Nathaniel Fry at Shakers, N. Y. May 26, 1892. Age 88 yrs. 9 mos. and 22 days. Sister Eliza M. Byram Sister Eliza M. Byram, at Pleasant Hill, Ky., in the eightieth year of her age. Sister Eliza came into the Society with her father when only ten years old. She has filled many places of trust, during her long life, and lias been one who gave her all to the cause. During the last years of her life, she has been an invalid, suffering much, and when the last hours came, she feared not to 'walk through the valley of the shadow of death.' J. W. S.
