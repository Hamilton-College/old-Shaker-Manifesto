Hard and Bitter Words —We all speak and write too many hard and bitter words. We try to break, not to mend the bruised reed. While we strive to be s trong in faith, let us not despise the faith of the weakest; for there will come a day to all of us when, in the giving way of the powers of nature, and the pouring in of the great water-floods, we may grasp at something which may hold, up and carry us over. And then, not how much we have searched out and know, not how much we have disputed and prevailed, will help us, but how much we have lived on Christ and imbibed his Spirit. — Dean Alford. — Ex.
