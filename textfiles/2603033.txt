Deaths. Bishop Pomeroy Bishop Pomeroy, at Hancock, Mass. Jan. 31, 1896. Age 84 years and 6 mo. He was a good and virtuous man, true to the cause he had espoused, and a kindly willing helper. He was well spoken of by all. E. B. Catherine Van Houten Catherine Van Houten, at Mount Lebanon, N. Y. Feb. 7, 1896. Age 78 years and 2 mo. H. R. A.
