Whitewash or cheap paint Four oz. lime10 lbs. whiting; 4 qts. skim-milk; 1 pt. soap suds. Color to suit. Put the lime in a stone jar ; pour on hot water to slack the lime, and then add milk enough to make it look like cream. Add the whiting and the remainder of the milk. Then stir in the soapsuds and the coloring matter. If to be used without coloring, a small teaspoonful of bluing will make it whiter.
