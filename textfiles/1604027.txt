Adaline Sears Adaline Sears, Feb. 12, 188G, at Center Family, Mt. Lebanon, N. Y. Age 54 yrs. G mo. and 10 days. She has been a faithful burden bearer in the family where she resided, for the past twenty-five years. She was universally beloved and respected, for her steadfast integrity, and unswerving devotion to Christian principles, and it is with the deepest regrets, that we part with her in the prime of life. E. A. S.
