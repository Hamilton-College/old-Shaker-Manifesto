The Ladies' Home Journal For the first time in his literary career Jerome K. Jerome, is about to write directly for an American audience. This work consists of a series of papers similar in vein to his 'Idle Thoughts of an Idle Fellow,' but addressed to American girls and women. The articles will begin shortly in The Ladies' Home Journal, which periodical will print the entire series.
