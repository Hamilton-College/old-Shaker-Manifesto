The first thing is to make your sermon plain... The first thing is to make your sermon plain. Rev. Blomfield preached on the text, 'The fool hath said in his heart, 'There is no God.' ' Wishing to find out how it pleased his people, he called a poor foolish man to the pulpit and asked him how he liked the sermon. The reply, which made Blomfield a sadder and a wiser man, was, 'Well, sir, I must say that I can't agree with you. In spite of all you've said, I think there must be a God.
