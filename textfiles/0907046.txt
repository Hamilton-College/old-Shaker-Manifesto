Prevention of Vice ' Thai well thought it very unfair to influence a child's mind by inculcating any opinions before it had come to years of discretion to choose for itself. I showed him my garden, and told him it was a botanical garden. ' How so?' said he; 'it is covered with weeds.' O, I replied, that is only because it has not yet come to its age of discretion and choice. The weeds you see have taken the liberty to grow, and thought it unfair in me to prejudice the soil in favor of roses and strawberries.'
