HOME TOPICS Graham crackers One pint sour cream, one half teaspoonful oda, one tablespoonful sugar, a little salt, tiffen with Graham flour sufficient to roll ut quite thin; cut in diamonds, and bake n a quick oven. If sour cream cannot be procured, use milk, adding a small piece of butter. Glassware Glassware.—Tumblers that have had milk n them ought never to be put into hot water. Washing Washing.—Borax, put in the water used for washing ginghams and highly colored prints, will preserve the colors.
