Gazing rudely at strangers. 7. Leaving a stranger without a seat. 8. Making yourself hero of your own story. 9. Reading aloud in company without being asked. 10. Spitting about the house- -smoking or chewing. 11. Leaving church before worship is over. 12. Whispering or laughing in the house of God. 13. A want of respect and reverence for seniors. 14. Correcting older persons than yourseli especially parents. 15. Receiving a present without an expression of gratitude. 16. Not listening to what one is saying in company. 17. Commencing to eat as soon as you get to the table. 18. Answering questions that have been put to others. 19. Commencing talking before others have finished speaking. 20.
