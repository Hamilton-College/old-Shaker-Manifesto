ILLITERACY IN MASSACHUSETTS The whole number of illiterates in this State in 1885 was 122,263, of which 45,550 are males and 70,713 females. Of the whole number, 96 770 are wholly illiterate, that is, can neither read nor write, and 25,360 can read but not write. There are 133 who make the singular claim that they can write but not read. The number of illiterates between the ages 10 and 13, both inclusive, was 2158; between 14 and 19, 4303; 20 and 29, 8747; 30 and 49, 16,544; fifty years and over, 13,774.
