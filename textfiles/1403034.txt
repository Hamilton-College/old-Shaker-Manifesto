Books and Papers Anti-masonic Scrap Book The National. Christian Association of Chicago, 111., chartered in 1874 ' To expose, withstand and remove secret societies, Freemasonry in particular * * * ' has compiled a selection of its tracts and published them in a neat pamphlet of 176 pages, under the title of ' Anti-masonic Scrap Book. ' In this series of tracts will be found the opinions oi such men as Hon. J. Q. Adams, Win. H. Seward, James Madison, Daniel Webster, Itich and Rush, John Hancock, Millard Fillinore, Chief Justice Marshall, Howard Crosby, D. L. Moody and others. Price 25c per copy.
