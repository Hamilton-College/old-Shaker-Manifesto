Eldress Harriet Storer Eldress Harriet Storer, at Enfield, Ct., June 6, 1890. Age 73 yrs. 4 mo. and 6 days. For more than sixty-five years, Eldress Harriet has lived with Believers, and has ever been loyal to the cause, true to her friends and faithful and conscientious through her whole life. She was dearly beloved by all, and we shall sadly miss her wise, cheerful and well-timed remarks. S. C.
