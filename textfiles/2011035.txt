KIND WORDS. It affords me pleasure to say that our interesting magazine... Ballston Spa, Saratoga Co., N. Y. Oct. 1890. It affords me pleasure to say that our interesting magazine always proves a very welcome visitor to our home, for aside of the beautiful sentiments contained in its pages, is the knowledge that I am personally acquainted with several of its gifted contributors. Therefore I should certainly miss our little spiritual messenger very much. Kindly your sister in the faith, Sophia Wayne.
