Books and Papers. The Phrenological Journal and Science of Health The Phrenological Journal and Science of Health for April has in its Contents a portrait of Andrew Carnegie the successful manufacturer; A Letter to the British Medical Journal; Notable people of the Da,y, No. 30; Studies from Lavater; Practical Phrenology; Sketches of Phrenological Biography; Child Culture; Science of Health; Notes in Science and Industry, etc., etc. Fowler &amp; Wells Co., 775 Broadway, N. Y. The Journal of Hygeio-Therapy The Journal of Hygeio Therapy has in its Contents for April, Signs of the Medical Times; Calisthenics, No. 2; A Prepared Body; Anti-Vaccination Department; Vaccination; Department of Horticulture; Farm Life for the Young; Raspberry Culture; The care of Grapevines; Editorial Department; The Most Exhausting Labor; Miscellaneous Department; The Undesirability of Flesh Meat, No. 1, etc., etc. Published by Dr. T. V. Gifford &amp; Co., Kokomo, Ind. The Philadelphia Musical Journal The Philadelphia Musical Journal for April, has in its Contents the portrait of Clara E. Thorns the distinguished pianist; Love of Music; Educational Comments; Local Musical Notes; Life in a Church Choir; Open Questions; Nasal Breathing in Singing; The Violin; Educational Department; Musical News, etc., etc. Gould &amp; Woolley, 1416 Chestnut St., Philadelphia, Pa.
