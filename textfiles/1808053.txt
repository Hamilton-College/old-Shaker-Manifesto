The Manifesto published by the 'United Societies' at Canterbury, N. H.... THE MANIFESTO published by the 'United Societies,' at Canterbury, N. H.. finds its way to our table occasionally. It is full of Christian spirituality, and we wel come it as we welcome the touch of a loving hand, and feel better within after having read it. Our subscribers should send for a copy.
