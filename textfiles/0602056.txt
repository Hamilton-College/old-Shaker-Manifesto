HOW TO KEEP THE DEVIL OUT E. T. LEGGETT What famous plans by man unfurled, Each era brings about, Whose promise is to save the world, And keep the Devil out. How hope alternates with despair, Ab each new plan is tried, And with 'Old Evil' waiting there, Its merits to decide. Thus all our plans, like autumn leaves, Have fallen one by one, And Satan homage still receives, As when the world began: There's but one only plan that would Keep out this Son of Sin, 'Tis fill the heart so full of good, The Devil can't get in.
