Jerusha Truair Jerusha Truair, at Shakers N. Y. June 6, 1898. Age 81 years 7 mo. and 27 days. She had been with Believers for sixtysix years, first with the Society at Sodus, with whom she removed to Groveland, thence to Shakers, N. Y. She has been an honest, industrious, faithful soul, and has lived a long life of usefulness. E. E. W.
