Exorcising Spirits f. w. e. [NOTE.] . I had the following statement from the lips of Dr. Phelps of Stamford, Conn., which is a village of retired Gospel ministers: When, beyond a doubt, his two children became Spiritual mediums — the wonder of the whole country —• a meeting of the clergy and their deacons was convened to exorcise Spirits, as they had no legal power to kill the mediums. Each one came armed with the sacred Word of God — a holy Bible. Whilst sitting all around the room, with the Bibles in their laps, the books began, of their own accord, to fly from one's lap at the head of one of the ministers across the room.
