Petroleum has a strong preservative power... Petroleum has a strong preservative power, converting soft, perishable woods to the durability of red cedar. It improves all farm implements, baskets, all wooden tools, as rakes, hoe handles, common waterpails or any wooden tool which is exposed to the weather. It may be found valuable, also, for rustic work, rustic furniture or chairs left upon a piazza. Give them a good coat of this oil occasionally. It will harden the wood, give them a dark color and make them last longer.
