Deaths. Paulina Pearsons Paulina Pearsons, at Shirley, Mass., May 19, 1893. Age 83 yrs. 2 mo. and 6 days. A member of the Society since her early youth, and for many years an active burden-bearer. J. W. Maria Hedrick Maria Hedrick, at Mt. Lebanon, N. Y. Age 67 yrs. 10 mo. and 12 days. Our Sister has spent fifty-seven years in the gospel work and has left this shady vale, loved and honored by all. Thou hast fought the glorious battle, Take the crown thy strife hath won, Don the robe of sweet rejoicing, Enter rest with work well done. J. E. S.
