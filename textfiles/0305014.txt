Dual. The latest originality in the way of newspaper titles is found, of all places in the world, among the Shakers of New Lebanon. For two years they have issued a journal called The Shaker, which was the organ of their sect throughout the country. Lately, however, they have recognized the Woman's Rights doctrine, by terming their paper The ' Shaker and Shakeress.' This is certainly a striking, if not a euphonious title. Buffalo Express.
