Deaths Jane Wing Jane Wing , at South Union, Ky., Nov. 20, 1889. Age 82 yrs. 4 mo. and 13 days. Sister jane came to this Society in 1811. Another veteran now has gone From earth to her eternal home, The clay we place beneath the sod, The spirit 'Wings' its way to God. H. L. E. Abigail Bennett Abigail Bennett , at Enfield, Conn., Nov. 20, 1889. Age 82 yrs. and 8 mo. Thy labors are ended, thy spirit set free, And the welcome of angels is now heard by thee; For a true child of Mother has joined the bright throng, To swell in sweet praises the eternal song. M.
