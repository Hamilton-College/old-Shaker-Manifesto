The Rev. Mr. Wells, secretary of the Ohio State Y. M. C. A., made the statement... The Rev. Mr. Wells, secretary of the Ohio State Y. M. C. A., made the statement on the evening of the 11th inst., to an audience of several hundred people, that ' the time is coming when Christians will love God so intensely that they will put to death even their own children who may be found holding and teaching heretical opinions.' Has the doctrine of the ' Vicarious Atonement' begun to bear such fruit as this, in these latter days of 1' boasted civilization ?' L. M. H. S.
