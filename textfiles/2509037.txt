The Sunday School Times The Sunday School Times of August 3 con tains a highly readable article by Dr. Charles S. Robinson on the much discussed and little understood question what constitutes 'Singableness in Music.' Dr. Robinson is the compiler of some of the best books of sacred song that have ever been published, and in this article be draws not only on his theoretical knowledge as a hymnologist and musician, but on his practical experience as a maker of music-books.
