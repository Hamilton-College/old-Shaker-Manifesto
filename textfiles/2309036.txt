Calvin Stebbins Calvin Stebbins at Enfield, Conn. June 13, 1893. Age 92 yrs. 8 mo. and 18 days. Br. Calvin entered the Community at the age of 13 years, and since that date has given to the cause, his whole heart. At the age of 82 he accidently fell on the ice and has not been able to walk since that date. He was been a patient sufferer. G. A.
