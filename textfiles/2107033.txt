Do not add condiments to food while cooking... Do not add condiments to food while cooking. If you must put butter, sugar, salt and other such ingredients in your food, do not do so until after the cooking is done; for if you do, you not only get the bad effects of the articles themselves, but the food is also rendered much harder to digest. The whole mass having become thoroughly permeated with these things, it is with difficulty that they are separated from the food and carried away. In many of the large, first class hotels they have adopted the French custom of preparing the food plain, and letting each one season to taste. The Journal of Hygeio-Therapy.
