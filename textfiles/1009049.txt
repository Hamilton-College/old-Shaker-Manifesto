Wash the tomatoes, put them into a kettle and mash as fine as possible; add the salt and spices and boil two hours, then strain through a sieve, carefully rubbing through the pulp; put back and boil; at the end of four hours remove; when cold bottle and seal ; keep in a cool dry place. When boiling it should be stirred almost constantly so that it may not burn. We also find it advisable to wire the corks.
