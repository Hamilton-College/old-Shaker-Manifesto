OUR EXCHANGES. The Religious Telescope The Keligiotjs Telescope of Dayton, Ohio, is an able advocate of the 'United Brethren in Christ.' Its editorial care is under the management of I. L. Kephart, D. D. A close application to the teachings found in the Telescope would establish a beautiful church of united Christian Brethren and Sisters.
