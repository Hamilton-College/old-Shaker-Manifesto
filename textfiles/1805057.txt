Clawson R. Middleton Clawson R. Middleton, at the Canaan Family, Col. Co., N. Y. Mar. 25, 1888. Age 85 yrs. 6 mo. and 6 days. Br. Clawson lived in the Society sixty one years, and in the Canaan Family thirty four years. He gave his whole life in devotion to the interests of his Brethren and Sisters, and to the gospel cause. G. W. C.
