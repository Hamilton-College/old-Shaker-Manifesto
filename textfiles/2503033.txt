Deaths. Eleanor Potter Eleanor Potter, at the Church family, Mt. Lebanon, N. Y.. January 21, 1895. Age 82 years, 9 mo. and 21 days. Sister Eleanor has for eighty years been a loved member of our family. Virtuous, intelligent and useful through a long life, when the end came, she 'wrapped the drapery of her couch about her, and lay down to pleasant dreams.' A. J. C.
