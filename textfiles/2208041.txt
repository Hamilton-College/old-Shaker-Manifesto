Deaths. Samantha Bowie Samantha Bowie, at Watervliet, N. Y., May 30, 1892. Age 53 yrs. 1 mo. and 23 days. J. B. Mary Page Mary Page, at Shaker Station, Conn. June 16, 1892. Age 21 yrs. 8 mo. and 14 days. Our kind little Sister passed from our sight Just when the life-bud promised to be A blossom of worth and beauty entwined With the graces of love and humility.
