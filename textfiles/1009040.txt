Spiritual harmonies Spiritual Harmonies: Being nearly one hundred popular hymns and songs, with a summary of the belief of Spiritualists, and a series of sensible and comforting readings for the sick room and funerals. A glance through this little work confirms our opinions of the necessity of such a book, and that a knowledge of the same will create an immediate and immense demand. Its compilation and renderings are the work of our friend and brother J. M. Peebles, and published and sold by Colby and Rich, Boston, Mass. Price 25 cents.
