The Separated Nation The Separated Nation, is book No. 19 of the Anti-Infidel Library. Pub. by H. L. Hastings, Boston, Mass. This little work of 224 pages, is a very interesting History of the Jewish Nation. 'In surveying this imperishable nation, which like the burning bush on Horeb seems to flourish amid the devouring flames, we may notice several marked peculiarities which distinguish the Jews from all other people.'
