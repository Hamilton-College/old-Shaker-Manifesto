ONLY THREE STEPS. A leaened divine one day accosted a simple-hearted Christian busy in his daily toil: 'Well, William, it is a long and hard way to Heaven, isn't it?' 'O no, sir,' was the ready answer; 'it is only three steps.'' 'Three steps! Why, how is that, William?' 'why, sir, nothing is plainer. First, step out of yourself; second, step into Christ; third, step into Heaven.' The astonished minister, years afterward, acknowledged his indebtedness to the poor rustic for one of the most instructive and comprehensive lessons in experimental theology. ï¿½Selected.
