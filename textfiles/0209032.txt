Deacon David Osborn had frequently met neighbor Camp, and compared their knowledge of the scriptures, until the Deacon was nearly out of patience. In a hurry, he was passing near the field where friend Camp was at work, when Camp, as usual, began : ' David, do you know what Haul says ?' ' Yea,' replied the Deacon, ' It is good for a man not to touch a woman ! ' ' No, no,' said Camp, ' I don't mean dat, not dat! '
