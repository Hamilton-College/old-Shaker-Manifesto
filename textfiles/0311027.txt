The more light admitted to apartments, the better for... The more light admitted to apartments, the better for those who occupy them. Light is as necessary to sound health as it is to vegetable life. Exclude it from plants, and the consequences are disastrous. They cannot be per fected without its vivifying influence. Let in the light often, and fresh air, too, or suffer the penalty of aches and pains, and long doctors' bills, which might have been avoided by more light.
