Catharine Vedder Catharine Vedder, at Shakers, Albany Co., N. Y., Nov. 9, 1891. Age 92 yrs. 9 mo. and 27 days. Sr. Catharine has lived in the Communitysince infancy, and has always been found in the path of duty. She was a pattern of righteousness and of her it can be said, 'Blessed are they that die in the Lord.' J. B.
