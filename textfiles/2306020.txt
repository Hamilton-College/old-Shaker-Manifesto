CONCISE HISTORY OF THE SHAKERS We shall soon have ready for distribution, a work of more than one hundred and thirty pages, under the title of a CONCISE HISTORY OF THE SHAKERS. The writer of this very interesting work, resides in the City of New York and gave publicity to the articles through the columns of the 'Manufacturer and Builder.' The book is well printed and liberally illustrated with views of the Shakers and their homes. Price in Cloth 75 cts. and in Leather with stiff covers, 60 cts. Postage free. Address H. C. Blikn, East Canterbury, N. H. We have omitted our page of music this month, as our space is limited, but have arranged to publish two pages of music in the next Manifesto.
