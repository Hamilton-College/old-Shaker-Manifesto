Deaths Polly Taylor, Dec. 18, 1883, at north family, Union Village, Ohio. Age, 78 yrs. Has been a member of the Society for sixty years and was a very faithful sister in the discharge of all her duties, both spiritual and temporal. O. C. H, Daniel L. Kidney, Dec. 17, 1883, at North Union, Ohio. Age, 64 yrs. 10 mo. and 25 days.
