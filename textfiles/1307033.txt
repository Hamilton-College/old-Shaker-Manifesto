TO MAKE PICKLES For 1 gall, cucumbers, take 1 qt. salt, with cold water sufficient to cover them. Let them remain in this brine three days, then remove from the brine, and pour over them weak vinegar, scalding hot. After four days, remove from this liquor, and pour over them good strong vinegar, heated, adding pepper, spice, mustard, &amp;c, to your taste.
