A Historical Introduction To The Peshitto Syriac New Testament A pamphlet of unusual Interest by H. L. Hastings of Boston has just come to us. It bears the title, A Historical Introduction To The Peshitto Syriac New Testament which was translated into English by the famous Biblical scholar James Murdock, S. T. D. The object of the Historical Introduction is to assist the student in acquiring a more thorough acquaintance of the work. In publishing this Introduction Mr. Hastings is rendering a valuable service to Biblical students, anil in fact, to all who are interested in securing an accurate account of the original manuscripts of the books of the Bible.
