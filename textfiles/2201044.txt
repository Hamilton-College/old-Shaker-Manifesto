Deaths Harriet Burgess Harriet Burgess , at Second Family, West Pittsfield, Mass. Nov. 21, 1891. Age 80 yrs. 2 mo. and 6 days. Sister Harriet came into the Society when fourteen years of age. In the soft twilight of morning Her freed spirit passed away To behold the glorious dawning Of a bright, eternal day. J. L. S. Harriet P. Hart Harriet P. Hart , at Harvard, Mass. Nov. 24, 1891. Age 57 yrs. 10 mo. and 27 days. Sister Harriet was called our artist Sister, and has lived in the Community for three years. She was an educated nurse and since being with us has given her life to the interests of our religious home. L. E. G.
