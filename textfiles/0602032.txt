A dyspeptic minister gorging his system with the rich food... A dyspeptic minister gorging his system with the rich food prepared by the 'sisters,' and taking no exercise, is a spectacle to make only infidels and undertakers rejoice. It was gravely proposed to inscribe on the tombstone of a gluttonous young minister, who was cut off by disease induced in this way: 'Died of going out to tea.' Science of Health
