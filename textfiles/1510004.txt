THE SECRET OF A HAPPY DAY Just to trust and yet to ask Guidance still; Take the trainings or the task As He will; Just to take the loss or gain As He sends it; Just to take the joy or pain As He lends it. Just to have in His dear hand Little things; All we cannot understand, All that stings : Just to let Him take the care Sorely pressing, Finding all we let Him bear Changed to blessing. This :s all! and yet the way Marked by Him who loves thee Secret of a happy day, [best] Secret of His promised rest. Selected.
