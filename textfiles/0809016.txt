LETTER OF ACCEPTANCE [We should have been pleased to have had the appended letter of Elder Evans inserted in our AUG. no. As it gives expression to the ideas of one of our prominent leaders, to be elaborated upon by him at the Liberal League Convention, the letter will be in- teresting. Elder Evans is a living, progressive man; and though his propositions are far in advance of his day, are they not demanded, desirable and true? Ed.]
