A young clergyman having preached for Dr. South, one day... A young clergyman having preached for Dr. South, one day, was anxious to get a word of applause for his labor of love. The grave doctor, however, did not introduce the subject, and his younger brother was obliged to bait the hook for him. 'I hope, sir,' he said, 'I did not weary your people by the length of my sermon today V' 'No, sir, not at all; nor by the depth either.' The young man was silent.
