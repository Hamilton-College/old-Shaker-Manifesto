Deaths. Phebe Van Houten Phebe Van Houten, at Mt. Lebanon, K Y. April 20, 1895. Age 77 years and 4 mo. Sister Phebe has by a long life of consecrated service won the sincere love and esteem of all who knew her. E. S. Julia H. Carpenter Julia H. Carpenter, at Pleasant Hill. Ky. May 29, 1895. Age 15 yrs. Sister Julia was received into the Community when she was two years of age, and has been 'faithful unto death.' Her ever kind and courteous manners made for her many friends. J. W. S.
