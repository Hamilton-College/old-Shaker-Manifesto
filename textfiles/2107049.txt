Deaths. Charlotte Priest Charlotte Priest at Harvard, Mass. May 25, 1891. Age 90 yrs. 1 mo. and 13 days. Sister Charlotte has had a long experience. The spirit of purity was a marked characteristic in all her actions. She came into the Community in early life and leaves a clean record of 'Well done, good and faithful servant, enter thou into the joy of thy Lord.' A. D. B. Amanda Boyd Amanda Boyd at South Union, Ky. Apr. 28, 1891. Age 35 years. Entered the Community in 1887. H. L. E. Julia Danow Julia Danow at Union Village, Ohio, June, 9., 1891. Age 79 yrs. 5 mo. and 27 days. An interested, unselfish, sincerely devoted Sister since early childhood. C. C.
