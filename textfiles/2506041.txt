Deaths. Mary Russell Mary Russell, at Shaker Station, Conn. Feb. 24, 1895. Age 84 years. Faithful unto death. D. Orcutt. John H. Parsell John H. Parsell, at Shaker Station, Ct. April 14, 1895. Age 75 years. Br. John had lived with us nine years and never was heard to speak ill of any one. D. Orcutt.
