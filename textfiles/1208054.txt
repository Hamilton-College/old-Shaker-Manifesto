The experiments of a famous Swedish chemist... The experiments of a famous Swedish chemist, prolonged over two years, making it definitely certain that separating cream by the centrifugal secures 10 per cent, more of it than any other process, while if the cream is at once churned what chemists and other experimenters pronounce the best lasting and best keeping butter is obtained ; the refuse— the skimmed milk and buttermilk, are sweet, that is, in their most valuable condition—and the milk has been in the course of a few hours turned into money. This appears to be the ultimate perfection of scientific butter-making. Ex.
