Composition Tea This almost universal remedy of the Shakers, and which has been sought for by so many thousands as a panacea for colds, coughs, lung irregularities and inflammations, is now made public: Take two pounds of bayberry root bark, one pound of the inner bark of hemlock, one pound of ginger, two ounces of cayenne pepper, all reduced to a powder and sifted through a fine sieve. Mix well together. Dose : one teaspoonful in half a teacup of hot water. Add milk and sugar to please the taste, and drink as warm as consistent.
