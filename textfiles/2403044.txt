Deaths. Rachel Russell Rachel Russell, at Watervliet, Ohio. Jan. 14, 1894. Age 84 yrs. 9 mo. and 20 days. Sister Rachel came to Watervliet with the Brethren and Sisters from North Union. For a long term of years, she was one of the most active and interested members, in bearing the burdens of the Community. A patient and charitable Sister, for whom must come the reward of 'Well Done.' H. W. F.
