TOMATO SOUP The following recipe for ' tomato soup ' is always timely, as canned tomatoes can be used in place of those growing in season : . One pint of canned tomatoes or four large raw ones, cut up fine. Add one quart of boiling water, and let them boil ten minutes, or until done. Remove from the stove and stir in one teaspoonful of soda. While foaming add one pint of sweet milk, salt and pepper and a small piece of butter. To be eaten hot with crackers, like oyster soup, to which it is almost equal. m. g. r.
