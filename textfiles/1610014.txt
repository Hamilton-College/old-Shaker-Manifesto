GRATITUDE. May our Heavenly Parents always be praised for an air that may be formed into pleasant sounds. Looks of love are sweet but kind words are far more dear. The former act upon the mind like moonbeams upon the water, but the latter fall cordially into it thence springing up in flowers and choice fruit, like seeds planted in the earth. It is said that the deaf Beethoven exclaimed, ' all the pleasures of sight and sense, all my eyes ever saw, would I give for one whisper to my heart.' Harriet Shepard.
