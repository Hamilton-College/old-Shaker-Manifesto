Selected Good news for Vegetarians Jews have refrained from the eating of oysters because a verse of scripture forbids the eating of creatures living in the water that 'have not fins or scales ;' but an English rabbi has released his 'flock' from this aggravating deprivation by declaring the oyster to be a plant.
