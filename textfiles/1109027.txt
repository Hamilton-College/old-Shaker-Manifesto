Attendance does not always indicate the true strength of a church. There are churches in New York and Brooklyn, Presbyterian and others, that we hear little of, that are doing more for Christianity and more for mankind than the Tabernacle, They are not based upon curiosity nor temporary excitement, but upon enduring faith and sincere devotion. These churches create no great stir, but they found missions, main tain numerous and costly churches, scatter Christian literature, and keep themselves respectable.
