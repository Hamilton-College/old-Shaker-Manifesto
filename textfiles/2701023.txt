Deaths William Thrasher William Thrasher , at Mt. Lebanon, N. Y. Sept. 24, 1896. Age 100 years 5 months and 1 day. S. A. C. Lydia Mariner Lydia Mariner , at Sabbathday Lake, Maine. Nov. 16, 1896. Age 83 years and 16 days. Sister Lydia has been a true and faithful member of this Society from early childhood. We shall miss her kind ministrations and loving words. A. G. M.
