The Ladles' Home Journal A NEW ART EDITOR. William Martin Johnson, who illustrated the 'Garfield' edition of 'Ben Hur' for the Harpers, and also their editions of 'The Cloister and the Hearth' and 'Hypatia,' becomes the art editor of The Ladles' Home Journal on June 1st, leaving New Y'ork to reside permanently in Philadelphia. Mr. Barton Cheyney, a clever newspaper man who has been attached to the press of Delaware and Pennsylvania is also added to the Journal's editorial staff as one of Mr. Bolt's principle associates.
