It is a very common thing for Christians to pray that the Lord will add His blessing to their works... It is a very common thing for Christians to pray that the Lord will add His blessing to their works, as if they were ahead and the Lord followed after. A Christian's daily prayer should be, that he may know what the Lord would have him do. A Christian's daily work should be the doing of what the Lord sets him at. There is never any doubt about the blessing, when that order of service is observed.
