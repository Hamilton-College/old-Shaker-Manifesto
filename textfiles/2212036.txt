Beloved Elder Henry:--I send a little article... Mount Lebanon, N. Y. Oct., 1892. Beloved Elder Henry:I send a little article which you may be interested to peruse. The Manifesto comes laden with good cheer and consolation, which truly lifts the soul to higher joys, and helps one to set out afresh on life's journey with more zeal to live closer to the faith of the gospel of Christ. This is the prayer of your friend. Phebe Van Houten.
