Deaths. Melinda Russell Melinda Russell at Watervliet, 0. Oct. 21, 1889. Age 83 yrs. Sister Melinda has been a faithful and devoted member from early youth. She was for many years one of the burden bearers in the Society at North Union. S. S. M. George Haffgesang George Haffgesang, at Watervliet, N. Y. Oct. 23, 1889. Age, 84 yrs. 1 mo. and 7 days. Br. George has lived in the Community twenty-nine years. O. B. Chancy Dibble Chancy Dibble at Watervliet, N. Y., Nov. 1, 1889. Age 67 yrs. 4 mo. and 19 days.
