Eldress Nancy E. Moore Eldress Nancy E. Moore at South Union, Ky., Dec. 5, 1889. Age 82 yrs. 4 mo. and 5 days. Eldress Nancy came into the Society on the 11th. of Nov. 1811, and has been a faithful soul ever since. She leaves the earth for heaven above, Where saints and angels dwell in love. H. L. E.
