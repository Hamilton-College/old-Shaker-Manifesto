Clarissa Patterson Clarissa Patterson, at Union Village, O. Sept. 27, 1895. Age 94 years, 0 mo. and 15 days. She has lived among Believers ninety years. She has been truly faithful and zealous to maintain the Gospel by words and by works. She occupied almost every position of care and burden in the Society with great honor and success and always with the most scrupulous regard to justice, uprightness and sincerity. She has left in the family a sweet savor of good deeds, which will long remain to bless those who may choose to follow in her footsteps, righteousness and true holiness. O. C. H.
