A writer in an exchange says... A whiter in an exchange says: ' In one gutter I saw a pig; in the other the semblance of a man. The pig was sober; the man was drunk. The pig had a ring in his nose ; the man had one on his finger. The pig grunted; so did the man. And I said aloud, ' We are known by the company we keep; ' and the pig heard me and walked away, ashamed to be seen in the company of a drunken man. ' Selected.
