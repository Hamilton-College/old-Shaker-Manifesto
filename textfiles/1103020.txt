Not Forgotten A few days ago we received a Five Dollar note from our kind Bro. Watson Andrews, who seems to believe, that the unsalaried position of the editor of The Manifesto needs now and then a kindly deed. The following accompanied the happy present : I'm too busy to sing, so instead of a song, I send you a sum, my dear ; If you must have a song, make a song of the sum, And wont that be the sum of a song ?
