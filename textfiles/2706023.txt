Deaths. William H. Bussell William H. Bussell at Shakers, N. Y. April 8, 1897. Age 87 yrs. and 4 mo. Br. William had been a member of the Community more than thirty years, and as a teacher had few equals. He was a Professor of languages before uniting with the Believers. H. DeG. Robert Clark Robert Clark, at East Canterbury, N. H. April 29, 1897. Age 80 years 10 mo. and 24 days.
