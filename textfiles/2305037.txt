Christian Lantz Christian Lantz, at Watervliet, (). March 19, 1893. Age 85 yrs. 1 mo. and 10 days. Br. Christian was born in Bernsburg, Germany, Feb. 9, 1808. He entered the Society at North Union, O., March, 1852 and moved to Watervliet, O., Oct. 1889. He was loved and respected by all who knew him. H. W. F.
